About

Studies show that the frequencies "White Noise" relax the brain and block out unwanted distractions. White noise includes all sound levels which include higher frequencies which can sometimes be harsh to the listener. Pink Noise on the other hand is more like the more laid back twin sister of the White Noise. With it's lack of high pitch frequencies studies show that Pink Noise gives off a calm effect that benefits relaxation and sleep. Strong winds, rushing water and even heavy rain have similarities to this frequency which is why those sounds are so soothing to the ear.
