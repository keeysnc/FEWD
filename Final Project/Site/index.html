About

Pink Noise is Mother Nature's language. It can be heard during a rainstorm, or while meditating near a waterfall. It’s calming and soothing effects are similar to white noise, but better. Have you ever felt the calm brought to you by listening to the rain tapping against your window? Or listing to the wind rustling the leaves in a tree? That’s all pink noise and it naturally gives you a better, deeper rest.
